Maps `classifier_name -> SeedEnsemble`. `predict_proba(X)` runs each `SeedEnsemble` and aggregates into a per-classifier mean probability; `predict_with_uncertainty` and consensus aggregation provide cross-classifier uncertainty. Factories: `from_any`, `from_json`, `from_dict`, `from_seed_ensembles`, `from_registry_dict`. Used as the universal "trained model" handoff between `TrainingModel` and `PredictionModel`.
