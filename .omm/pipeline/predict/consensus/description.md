`utils.array.aggregate_seed_probabilities` reduces the per-seed matrix into mean / std / confidence columns per classifier. A cross-classifier consensus (mean of per-classifier means) is added on top. Per-seed CSVs go under `prediction/results/{clf-slug}/`, the aggregated result lands on `self.results`, and the forecast figure is written under `prediction/figures/forecast_{start}_{end}.{png,pdf}`.
